## What it does

Yalla Video is a desktop app that downloads videos from YouTube and over a thousand other sites. Grab a single video, an entire playlist, or a full channel — with filters, search, and bulk selection.

- **Any quality up to 8K** — video, audio-only, or both
- **Formats** — MP4, MKV, MP3, M4A, WAV, and more
- **Subtitles** — embedded or as sidecar SRT/VTT files, in any available language
- **Smart Mode** picks the best format automatically; **manual mode** gives full control over codec, frame rate, and resolution
- **Parallel downloads** with bandwidth limits, scheduling, auto-retry, pause/resume
- **Clipboard-aware tray** for one-click captures from your browser
- **Proxy/SOCKS support** and browser cookie import
- **12 languages**, light/dark/themed UI
- **No telemetry, no account required**

Under the hood: `yt-dlp` and `ffmpeg`, kept up to date automatically in the background — no setup required.
